What is a hackathon?

Hackathon /ˈhæk əˌθɒn/ and game jam are the general terms for an event where a group of people try and build something new with technology, programming, creative skills. It's not to be compared to a LAN party.

Zerosleep.dk is my personal calendar of all the game jams, hackathons and competitions I could possibly attend. No online-only jams, only events that takes place IRL.
